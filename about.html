About Appalachian Adventures

Appalachian Adventures is a premier outdoor tour company dedicated to showcasing the breathtaking beauty of the Appalachian Mountains. With a focus on providing unforgettable experiences, we offer a range of guided tours that cater to adventurers of all skill levels. From invigorating day hikes that explore stunning vistas and hidden trails to immersive week-long hikes that include camping under the stars, Appalachian Adventures ensures that every participant connects deeply with the natural environment. Our knowledgeable guides bring a wealth of experience and a passion for the outdoors, enhancing the journey with insights into the region's rich history and diverse ecology.


For those seeking a more immersive experience, Appalachian Adventures also offers week-long hiking trips that combine camping and canoeing. This unique blend allows participants to explore the tranquil waterways of the Appalachians while also trekking through its majestic mountains. Campfires, shared stories, and the camaraderie of fellow adventurers create lasting memories. Each trip is meticulously planned to provide a balance of challenge and enjoyment, making it ideal for families, friends, or solo travelers looking to bond with nature and each other.


Safety and sustainability are at the heart of Appalachian Adventures’ mission. We emphasize environmentally responsible practices, ensuring that the natural beauty of the Appalachians is preserved for future generations. By limiting group sizes and following Leave No Trace principles, we provide an intimate experience while minimizing impact on the delicate ecosystems. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a first-time explorer, Appalachian Adventures invites you to step into the wild and discover the enchanting allure of the Appalachian Mountains.

Meet the Team

Elana - Founder & Lead Guide

Elana has been exploring the Appalachian Mountains for over 20 years. With a deep love for nature and a wealth of experience in outdoor survival, Elana founded Appalachian Adventures to share her passion with others. She leads many of the immersive week-long hikes, offering insights into the history, flora, and fauna of the region.

Brooke - Operations Manager
