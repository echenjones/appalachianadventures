Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

What is the Appalachian Trail?

The Appalachian Trail is a long-distance hiking trail stretching across the eastern United States from Georgia to Maine.

How long does it take to complete a thru-hike?

A typical thru-hike can take 5 to 7 months depending on pace and conditions.

Do I need to be an experienced hiker?

Our tours are designed for both beginners and experienced hikers, with varying difficulty levels.

Booking and Payment

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly on our website by selecting your preferred tour and date, and completing the booking form.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

Is there a deposit required?

Yes, a 20% deposit is required to secure your booking.

Preparation and Gear

What gear do I need to bring?

We provide a full packing list after booking. Basic hiking gear such as boots, backpacks, and clothing are essential.

Do you provide camping equipment?

Yes, we provide tents, cooking gear, and sleeping bags for overnight tours.

How should I prepare for a hike?

We recommend conditioning with regular walks or hikes, increasing distance and elevation gradually before your tour.
