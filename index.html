About Us

Appalachian Adventures is your gateway to exploring the stunning landscapes of the Appalachian Mountains through expertly guided hiking tours. With a passion for the great outdoors, we offer a range of experiences tailored to all skill levels, from leisurely day hikes that showcase incredible vistas, to immersive week-long treks that allow participants to camp under the stars. Our knowledgeable guides share insights into the region’s rich flora, fauna, and history, ensuring every hike is both educational and exhilarating. Whether you’re seeking a quick escape or an extended adventure, Appalachian Adventures promises unforgettable journeys in one of America’s most beautiful natural playgrounds.   Read more

Our Tours

Appalachian Adventures offers a variety of immersive outdoor experiences designed to connect adventurers with the breathtaking beauty of the Appalachian Mountains. Our day hikes provide the perfect opportunity for those looking to explore scenic trails and enjoy the natural landscape without an overnight commitment. For those seeking a deeper adventure, our week-long hikes combine breathtaking trekking with camping under the stars, allowing participants to fully immerse themselves in nature. Additionally, our canoeing and camping hikes blend the serenity of paddling with the thrill of camping, offering a unique way to explore the region's waterways.
